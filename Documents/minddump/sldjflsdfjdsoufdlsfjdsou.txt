Contradiction:
a) He who is changeable: is unreliable.
b) He who is not changeable: is stubborn, stupid, and arguably dead.

(a) Cleopatra tells Mark Antoni that he has a very changeable way about him. It is meant as an insult, and he proves her right moments later.
(b) Crow's Zero 1 intro song (which imo is the best part of all 3 movies) essentially says: if you aren't changing, you're dead.
